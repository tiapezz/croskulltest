What is CroSKull

It’s Christmas Eve night. In the Bonesville village, children are anxiously awaiting the arrival of Santa Claus, families toast around tables laden and Christmas lights twinkle everywhere. Only the grumpy and lonely Doctor Jiiko doesn't like to celebrate Christmas. In fact, while everyone’s cooking sweets and delicacies, he creates potions. And like all the famous villains, what better moment than Christmas Eve to bring his project "to life"? 6666 gloomy Croskulls are ready to invade the streets of the city!

The Croskull is a collection of 6,666 uniquely generated NFTs stored on the Cronos Chain. Each Croskull NFT is based on 6+ attributes with different rarities. The Croskull will not simply be pfp, but will also be the key to the experiments of Doctor Jiiko's laboratory.
